about us

‘CHARUTA’ is a General Construction Company, with an additional window of Interior Decoration and Renovation of buildings. We emerged in the market as the pioneer in the building interior decoration and supplier of furniture both imported and local. We had the opportunity of working in the construction and interior decoration works of several high commissions and embassies that are located in the Diplomatic Enclave, Dhaka. We expanded its business into real estate, ‘Charuta Homes Ltd.’ and successfully completed a number of luxurious apartment buildings in Gulshan & Baridhara, and Mohakhali DOHS, Dhaka. Read More
